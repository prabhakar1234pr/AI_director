fix(gemini): make token expiry timezone-aware (500 on /api/generate-images)

google-auth populates credentials.expiry as a naive datetime in UTC.
Comparing it against datetime.now(timezone.utc) (aware) raised
"can't compare offset-naive and offset-aware datetimes" the next
time the token cache was checked, surfacing as a 500 from
/api/generate-images.

Attach UTC tzinfo to the naive expiry before caching so subsequent
_token_is_valid() comparisons stay homogeneous.
